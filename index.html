ABOUT US

Better Games Studio Pty Ltd is an Australian mobile game publisher dedicated to delivering engaging and high-quality gaming experiences to players worldwide. Through a long-term strategic partnership with Better Games Studio, Islamabad, Pakistan, the company oversees the publishing, growth, monetization, and global distribution of its gaming portfolio. Together, the two organizations have successfully launched

and operated multiple commercially successful mobile games, reaching millions of players globally and generating significant revenue across international markets. By combining world-class publishing expertise with full-cycle game development capabilities, Better Games Studio Pty Ltd continues to expand its presence in the global mobile gaming industry while delivering innovative and player-focused entertainment experiences.
